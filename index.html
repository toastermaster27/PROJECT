About Us

The Riviera Theatre is a 370 seat venue in Three Rivers, Michigan. Located in our beautiful, historic downtown, it has been meticulously renovated and is now being used for films, concerts, live performance, and other special events. Built in 1911, it is the oldest movie theatre in the south-west Michigan area.
